# TelcoSec Labs

![TelcoSec Labs Profile Banner](https://img.shields.io/badge/TelcoSec-Security_Testing_Labs-0050FF?style=for-the-badge&logo=shield&logoColor=white)

> **Advanced Security Research & Education in Telecommunications Infrastructure.**

TelcoSec Labs is a community-driven initiative focused on auditing, securing, and educating on the complexities of modern and legacy telecommunications networks. From SS7 and Diameter to 5G SBA and O-RAN, we provide the tools and knowledge to explore the signaling plane.

## üî¨ Core Research Focus

Our work is divided into specialized labs, ensuring a modular approach to telecommunications security:

### üì° Network & Signaling
- **Core Network Security**: SS7, Diameter, and 5G SBA protocol testing.
- **RAN Security**: O-RAN architectures and air interface vulnerabilities.
- **SIM/eSIM**: Profile management and remote provisioning security.

### üì± Device & Operating Systems
- **Baseband Research**: Firmware analysis and RIL/QMI vulnerabilities.
- **TEE Security**: TrustZone analysis and secure boot auditing.
